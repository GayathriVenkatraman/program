# Lost at Sea

If the online link does not work, you can run the exercise yourself.

## Steps

1. **Fork the repository**
   Go to [lost-at-sea-exercise](https://github.com/HackYourFuture-CPH/lost-at-sea-exercise) and click **Fork**. This creates a copy in your GitHub account.

2. **Deploy on Vercel**
   - Sign in at [vercel.com](https://vercel.com) (use **Continue with GitHub**).
   - Click **Add New** → **Project**.
   - Choose your forked repo `lost-at-sea-exercise` and click **Import**.
   - Leave the default settings and click **Deploy**.
   - When the build finishes, open your project’s URL and use it for the exercise.

You now have your own “Lost at Sea” app to rank the 15 items.
